Information

   

We will play through games, Roblox to be exact. In this game, Dress to Impress, we will have numerous contents aiming to motivate and enhance an individual's creativity and passion for fashion.

   

The main reason why we chose to play Dress to Impress is because of its wide fan base and excellent content creators with a similar goal help enhance creativity. With the span of just 5 minutes, we will create a fashionista model in the game with people judging and rating each outfit, aiming to win the champion of each round.
